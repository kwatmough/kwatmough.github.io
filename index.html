Welcome

Hello, and welcome to the home of A Whisky Pleb. Here, I (a whisky pleb) will post reviews of those whiskies which I am lucky (or unlucky) enough to get a chance to try. My budget is the only limit, so join me as I work my way through bottom-shelf whiskies.

Each review will follow the following format:
  • Pour whisky.
  • Take a real good whiff
  • Drink
  • Drink more
  • Continue as above
  • Wait, I was supposed to review this stuff, wasn't I?
  • Well crap.
